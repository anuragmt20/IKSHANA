IKSHANA is a nonprofit organization founded in 2023 as a first responder to disasters.

Throughout history, natural disasters and humanitarian crises have brought out the humanity in all of us. Mankind bands together to support their fellow human beings in their time of need.
IKSHANA was founded to provide hope via a new model for disaster response, one that meets modern crises head-on with an innovative, adaptable, and scalable approach. One that harnesses strategic partnerships and empowers local populations in the rebuilding effort.
Our work is made possible entirely by the generous donors and volunteers, who we thank from the bottom of our heart.
